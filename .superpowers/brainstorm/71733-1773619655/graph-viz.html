A — Sigma.js (Recommended)

Fast WebGL rendering for large graphs. Click a node → it becomes the new center (what PyVis couldn't do). Drag, zoom, hover tooltips. Best performance at 200+ nodes.

Force simulation physics · drag · pin
Physics-based — nodes repel, edges attract. Can pin nodes by dragging.

B — D3 Force Graph

Physics simulation — nodes repel each other, edges pull them together. More organic feel. Fully customizable but more complex to build. Can get messy with 50+ nodes.

Grid / Concentric layout structured · clean · stable
Concentric rings by score — center node surrounded by ranked pairings.

C — Cytoscape.js

Structured layout algorithms (concentric, breadth-first). Nodes arranged by score into rings around the center ingredient. Less chaotic than force simulation, very readable.
